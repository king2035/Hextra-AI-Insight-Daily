### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on what's happening on our Service and to stash some info. These tracking goodies include beacons, tags, and scripts. They help us gather and follow information, plus they let us boost and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file that lands on your Device. You can tell your browser to ditch all Cookies or to give you a nudge when one's about to be sent. Heads up though: if you don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not work as smoothly. Our Service probably uses Cookies, unless you've adjusted your browser settings to give them the boot.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). These cool little tools let the Company, for instance, count who's visited those pages or opened an email. They're also handy for other website stats, like seeing how popular a section is or double-checking system and server health.

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent ones stick around on your computer or mobile device even after you go offline. Session Cookies, though, vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving you the services available on the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help confirm who users are and stop any funny business with accounts. Without 'em, the services you're asking for just can't happen, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply tell us if you've given the thumbs-up to using Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website â€“ like keeping your login deets or language preference in mind. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized vibe and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you visit.

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and your options? Just hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section in this Privacy Policy.
