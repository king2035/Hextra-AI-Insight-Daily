### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're using Cookies and other similar tracking tech to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and to save certain information. These tracking tools include things like beacons, tags, and scripts ‚Äì all designed to gather and track data, helping us fine-tune and analyze our Service. Here's a peek at what we use:

-   **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is just a tiny file that gets popped onto Your Device. You can totally tell Your browser to reject all Cookies or to alert You whenever one's being sent. But here's the catch: if You don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not work for You. So, unless You've tweaked Your browser settings to give Cookies the cold shoulder, our Service will likely be using them.
-   **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (You might also hear them called clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). These little guys let us, for instance, count who's visited certain pages or opened an email. They're also used for other website stats, like tracking how popular a section is or checking that our systems and servers are running smoothly.

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on Your personal computer or mobile Device even after You're offline. Session Cookies? They vanish the second You close Your web browser. Poof! üí®

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the reasons we're about to lay out below:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for making sure You can get the services available on our Website and use all its cool features. They're like bouncers for Your account, helping us verify who You are and stopping any sneaky, fraudulent activity. If we didn't have these Cookies, we simply couldn't give You the services You're asking for ‚Äì they're strictly for that!

-   **Cookies Policy / Acceptance of Cookies Notice**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are here to remember if You've already given us the green light to use cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
