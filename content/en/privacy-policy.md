### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and store certain information. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts. These help us collect and track info, plus improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file placed on your Device. You can tell your browser to refuse all Cookies or to alert you when a Cookie is being sent. However, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Our Service might use Cookies unless you've adjusted your browser settings to decline them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These files let the Company, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website statistics (like recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stay on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted right when you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are essential for providing you with available services through the Website and enabling you to use some of its features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've requested can't be provided, and we only use them to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to identify if users have accepted the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies allow us to remember your choices when you use the Website, like recalling your login details or language preference. The goal of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every time you use the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding Cookies, check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
